Each SequenceElement (seAminoAcid, seModifiedAminoAcid, Gap) has to specify a confidence and the position within the Sequence (positionInSequence). Proofs are optional. However, the intention of DNMSO is to increase the transparency of de novo prediitons and, therefore, adding support from the spectrum is highly encouraged.
